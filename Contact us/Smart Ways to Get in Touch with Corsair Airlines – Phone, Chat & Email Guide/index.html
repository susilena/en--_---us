📞 Corsair Airlines Customer Service – How to Reach Us

To contact Corsair Airlines customer service, you can reach them through various channels depending on your location and needs. ✈️

📱 Phone Numbers

  • 🇫🇷 From France: Dial 3917 (charged €0.35/min from landlines)
  • 🌎 International calls: +1-888-829-1117
  • 🇺🇸 North America: +1 888-657-8380
  • 🏢 Local offices in New York & Los Angeles: +1 888-657-8380

📧 Email Support

If you prefer written communication, send your inquiries to:

corsair.res@apg-ga.ca

Make sure to include your full name, booking reference, flight details, and a clear description of your issue to help us assist you efficiently.

⏰ Customer Service Hours

  • 🇫🇷 France: Daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM (local time)
  • 🇺🇸 North America: Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM (local time)

🛬 Airport Assistance

For immediate help with flight changes, cancellations, baggage issues, or check-in, visit our airport service counters. Having your booking reference and ID ready will help speed up the process. 🛄🛂
